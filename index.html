ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Yael Ziman, a graphic designer who is passionate about her work. I  am a self-starter and quick learner. I have a versatile skill set, in both graphic design and hand drawn pieces, with extraordinary creative thinking and project design abilities. I am able to work well independently, but I have always found that working as a team has helped me reach my greatest potential.

SIMPLE

Ziman Designs are simple and elegant, creating an eye-catching and clean product. I look over each project meticulously to make sure there are no mistakes.

UNIQUE

Every design I make has the promise of being unique and personalized. I always add a hand-drawn element in each design, giving me a deeper connection to each project I work on.

COLORFUL

Each project I make has the promise of being bold. Every design is made with a specific color palette to ensure continuity.
